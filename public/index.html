Time-series Analysis of Water Quality using data from multiple sources

The analysis of water quality is essential owing to increasing urbanization, pollution, population stressed resources, and lack of effective technological solutions. The water quality depends upon many different parameters. Fortunately, modern technology has equipped us with the ability to measure all these parameters efficiently. These factors can be measured using Geographic Information System (GIS), Remote Sensing (RS) and Internet of Things (IoT). The parameters, obtained, are used to measure water quality index (WQI) that indicates the quality of water for the given values of WQP
